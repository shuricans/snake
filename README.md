## Simple snake game. p5.js
No time for that, [just try it](https://shuricans.github.io/snake/).
### How to play?
- Use arrow keys too turn or speed select.
- Use space for start/restart.

### Values:

| mode | speed | food | bonus | timer (s) |
| :------------ | :------------ | :------------ | :------------ | :------------ |
| slow | x  | 10 | 100 | 6  |
| normal | 2x  | 20 | 200 | 4  |
| fast | 4x  | 40 | 400 | 2  |

### Your results:

|   | Score  | Speed  | Date  |
